ABOUT INOUT

What's a Hackathon?

A Hackathon is a collaboration of programmers and designers to code, design and build innovative solutions to real life problems, through extensive coding in a short time duration. Hackathons are the biggest thing to happen to education since the rise of the Internet. See this for a good explanation.

Your only constraint is: You only have 30 hours.

India's Premier Hackathon.

Hackathons provide a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology. People with technical backgrounds come together, form teams around a problem or idea, and collaboratively code a unique solution from scratch - these generally take shape in the form of websites, mobile apps, and robots. So prepare yourself for a weekend filled with learning new technologies, building amazing products and making new friends all within the span of 30 hours.

Everyone is welcome.

Whether you're making your first website, hacking Oculus Rift to censor real world ads, or creating something fun; InOut is for you. We'll have great workshops, mentors, and more! We will allow you to show your coding skills without getting worried about any mortal needs. We will take care of everything.

FAQ

What if I don't have a team or an idea?

If you don’t have a team now, don’t stress! We’ll have team formation and ideation events geared towards helping you find people to work with.

How much will it cost?

Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. InOut is free for all accepted hackers. We'll provide you with a weekend's worth of swag, meals, drinks, and snacks and a place to crash when you need a break from coding.

What's the application process like?

We're looking for creative and innovative people with a set of skills that can contribute to a project. We generally prefer people that have proven their abilities in previous projects, evident through Github links, project descriptions, or participation/awards in other hackathons.

How big can a team be?

This hackathon is strictly a team competition. No lone crusaders please. Max team size : 3 members.

Do you give travel reimbursements?

We will provide reimbursements after reviewing the requests received. If you are in Surat, you will get a free Uber ride to the venue and back.

When does registration open?

Registration will open on Sept. 15th 12:00am IST and will close on Oct. 18th 23:59pm IST. If you are accepted, you’ll have five days to confirm your attendance. Do Sign up for our updates!

Who can attend?

All the coolest people interested in tech will be there. Open to all! Any undergraduate and graduate student or Developers from anywhere in India and outside.

Once I get accepted, what do I need to bring?

Laptop and charger, cellphone and charger, extension, water-bottle, student ID/employee ID, a sleeping bag if needed and nerdy t-shirt!

What do i get by attending InOut?

Pretty much everything from socializing with fellow hacker community to internships/job offers from coolest tech companies. And yeah awesome prizes(worth 3 lakhs) too!

What if this FAQ didn’t answer my questions?

Please email us at mail@hackinout.co if you have any concerns at all! We’re friendly, and we’d love to help you out!
